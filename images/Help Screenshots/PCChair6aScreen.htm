Instructions

In this page you can view all the busy schedule posted by every member of the promotion committee. The increase in the numbers in the grid below represents the number of member busy at the specific hour. For e.g. if a number in a slot is 4 then there are 4 members busy at this hour. A Call for Meeting Button appears in a green Color when the time Slot is free and none of the members is busy at this time.

You can press this green Color Button to open an email template for Call for Meeting which will have the meeting time according to the time slot of the pressed Button.

Another way to call for meeting is to click on the Blue Color Call for Meeting Button below . Here, the meeting time by default will be Sunday 7:00 AM of the Busy week. You can change the meeting time according to your wish.
